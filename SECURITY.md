# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please email [lukema95@gmail.com](mailto:lukema95@gmail.com) with a detailed report. We will acknowledge receipt within 3 business days.

Please do not disclose security issues publicly until we have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and release fixes.

## Disclosure Process

1. We confirm the vulnerability and determine severity.
2. We work with you to understand and remediate the issue.
3. We release a fix and publicly acknowledge the reporter, unless anonymity is requested.

Thank you for helping keep Art Flip and its users safe.
